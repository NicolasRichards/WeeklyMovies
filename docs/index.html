Privacy Policy

Weekly Movies  ·  Effective June 2025

Weekly Movies is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

What We Don't Collect

  • No account or registration is required to use the app.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personal data.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking of any kind.

Your TMDb API Key

Weekly Movies uses The Movie Database (TMDb) to fetch movie data. You provide your own free TMDb API key, which is stored exclusively in your device's secure Keychain. It is never transmitted to us or any third party. All requests go directly from your device to TMDb's servers under TMDb's own privacy policy.

Your Watchlist & Seen List

Films you save to your Watchlist or mark as seen — along with any personal star ratings — are stored privately in your own iCloud account via iCloud Key-Value Storage. This data is synced across your devices using your Apple ID and is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We have no access to it.

Third-Party Services

  • TMDb — movie data. See their privacy policy.
  • Apple iCloud — watchlist sync. See Apple's privacy policy.

Children's Privacy

Weekly Movies does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date.
